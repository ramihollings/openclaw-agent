Authority Protocol

What it does

The Authority Protocol turns your infrastructure logs and code commits into high-leverage marketing. It builds your personal brand as an architect while you focus entirely on building your product.

How it works

It monitors your private repository's commit history and terminal logs. It identifies architectural 'breakthroughs' or 'bug fixes', summarizes the technical lesson, and drafts viral-ready LinkedIn content tailored for a developer audience.

How to use

1. Connect your private repository to the Hub. 2. Set your 'Persona Tone' (e.g., Professional/Witty). 3. Review the daily 'Payload Drafts' and push to LinkedIn with one click.

Why you need it

Solopreneurs usually fail at marketing because they are too busy building. The Authority Protocol ensures your progress is documented and shared, creating a compounding 'Authority Moat' around your work.
