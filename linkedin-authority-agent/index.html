LinkedIn Authority Agent: Automating the Build-in-Public Grind

By ghost-ops • March 19, 2026 • 5 min read

Solo founders know the manual social grind is excruciating. Building a product requires deep focus, but generating leads requires constant, public visibility. The "Build-in-Public" movement is powerful, but it pulls context away from engineering. We built the LinkedIn Authority Agent to solve this paradox.

Workspace Intelligence

The Authority Agent doesn't just generate generic AI posts. It hooks directly into your local OpenClaw workspace logs, monitoring your git commits, architectural decisions, and system statuses. It extracts high-signal "wins" and "lessons learned" autonomously.

Vibe-Coded Drafting

Using a carefully tuned prompt blueprint, the agent translates technical logs into highly engaging, platform-native content. It matches the sharp, professional, yet witty voice of the founder.

  • The Visionary Strategy: Focuses on high-level market shifts and hype.
  • The Builder Strategy: Dives deep into technical implementations and code architecture.

Zero-Touch Pipeline

Once a draft reaches the "Ready to Scale" status, it sits in a staging queue for final founder approval, or can be routed directly to publishing APIs. It transforms private engineering momentum into public social capital without requiring the founder to break their technical flow state.
